Welcome to the Future of Music Mentorship Portal

This is the central portal for the Future of Music Mentorship Program, designed to support youth ages 13–18 in St. Louis through entrepreneurship, academics, and personal growth. Here, mentees can submit their weekly goals and schedule check-ins with their mentors.

Program Overview

Purpose: Support youth ages 13–18 in entrepreneurship, academics, and personal growth.

Mission Statement: To empower youth in St. Louis to build leadership and business skills through peer mentorship and real-world experience.

Target Audience: Youth ages 13–18 in the St. Louis region, especially those interested in business or needing academic guidance.

Program Types

  • Entrepreneurship Mentorship
  • Academic Mentorship
  • General / Personal Development Mentorship

Format

  • 1-on-1 mentorship (Virtual / Phone Calls)
  • Weekly meetings
  • Length: 3 months

Mentors

  • Executive Director (Aereon): All-round mentor
  • Managing Director (Qwentin): All-round mentor
  • Program Coach (Gabriel): Academic and Personal Development mentor
