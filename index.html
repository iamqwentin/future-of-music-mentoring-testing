Welcome to the Future of Music Mentorship Portal

This is the central portal for the Future of Music Mentorship Program, supporting youth ages 13–18 in St. Louis through entrepreneurship, academics, and personal growth.

Program Overview

Purpose: Support youth ages 13–18 in entrepreneurship, academics, and personal growth.

Mission Statement: To empower youth in St. Louis to build leadership and business skills through peer mentorship and real-world experience.

Target Audience: Youth ages 13–18 in the St. Louis region.

Program Types

  • Entrepreneurship Mentorship
  • Academic Mentorship
  • General / Personal Development Mentorship

Format

  • 1-on-1 mentorship (Virtual / Phone)
  • Weekly meetings
  • 3-month program
